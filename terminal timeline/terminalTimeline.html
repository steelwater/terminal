Alpha Terminal

Where: Earth

1940 AD: Alpha Terminal was written in Assembly Language.

Terminal 0

Where: Earth

1954 AD: Terminal 0 was written in Basic Programming Language.

Terminal 1

Where: Earth

1980 AD: Terminal 1 was a minimalist terminal used for computer maintenance jobs. It used a text menu interface to connect to different networks and computers.

Star Terminal

Where: Earth: Philippines

1992 AD: Star Terminal was used to analyze the position of stars and found the planet M.456

Terminal 582

Where: Earth: Philippines

33rd Century AD: LightTech Corp. placed the Terminals in a capsule and burried it in anomalous areas like the Bermuda Triangle. Popular Scientific Theory states that these anomalous areas are worm holes that defy Physics` definition of Space and Time.

Terminal 582 was one of the terminals placed in a classified anomalous area.

Terminal 582 A.I. codename: Prompt
Prime Directive: Hack and Wipe Star Terminal to prevent the discovery of planet M.456. It will try to complete its mission as soon as it connects to the Internet.

Terminal 934

Where: Earth: South Africa

2038 AD: Merchants used Terminal 934 to deploy Gatherers.

pHT

Where: Earth

Unknown Future - pHT Personnel HyperSpace Terminal - was used by space service personnel to monitor vital stats while working in Space Ships. It was modified to interface with Ship Computers to retrieve information and control ship functions.
